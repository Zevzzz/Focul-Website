   _Focul User Guide

What is Focul: Focul is a computer application designed to help users who struggle with focusing or staying on task. It was especially designed for those who struggle with ADHD related focus issues. By using body landmark detection and a personalized neural network trained on each user's unique body language and body focus cues, Focul recognizes signs of distraction and gently reminds users to refocus. Unlike many other productivity apps, Focul is truly personalized for each user, adapting to unique body language and habits, making it an intuitive and personal tool for focusing.

System requirements: A windows PC/Laptop with a webcam.

Privacy: Once downloaded, Focul does not send or download any information over the Internet, it runs locally and does not use the Internet at all, so there should not be any privacy concerns.

Current Version: Beta. The current version is in early experimental, we intended to use it to gather user feedback, please bear with us the rudimental UI. It has some cool cutting edge technology such as AI (deep neural network and body gesture estimation), so you may find it fun to play with.

How to Download & Run Focul

(Average 3 minutes):

  1. Download Focul.zip from this Google Drive link. It is 1.23GB, mostly because there are a few large Google and other AI libraries.
  2. You may receive a warning that Google Drive Cant scan file for viruses, click on Download anyway.

  1. Go to your download folder on your computer
  2. Unzip Focul.zip (Right click on Focul.zip and click on Extract All)
  3. Go to the unzipped folder (usually Focul), and double click on main (or main.exe) to launch the Focul app. You may  get a windows alert (depending on your setting) that looks like the following. Dont worry, this is because the version hasnt been digitally signed and this is normal, click on More Info link then Run anyway button.

How to use it:

  1. It takes a few seconds to launch. It will first launch a black terminal used for debugging, and the main app UI right after. You can ignore the debug terminal but feel free to scan through for fun, you only need to focus on the App UI with 4 buttons.

  1. Record Focused Video. Click on the first button, Record Focus Video, the app will start to record 60 seconds of video and show the following webcam window, this will be used to train the AI engine, essentially teaching the engine on your focused gestures during study time. Please find a gesture that you usually use during study time, like looking at your monitor, or look at your book. Feel free to record multiple gestures within the 60 seconds window, and you can click on the button again to re-record.

  1. Record Unfocused Video. Click on the second button, Record Unfocused Video, the app will start to record 60 seconds of video and show the follow webcam window. This will also be used to train the AI engine, so it knows some patterns when you are not focused on study, like playing with your phone, looking around and etc. Feel free to record multiple gestures within the 60 seconds window, and you can click on the button again to re-record.

  1. Train AI model. Click on the 3rd button Train Model with Recordings, this will trigger the AI engine to train it self (learn your gestures) based on the recorded videos. The training will take a few seconds and a popup will indicate it is completed. Click on OK to dismiss the pooup.

  1. Now you are ready to use it! Click on the 4th button, start Monitoring Focus to start. A webcam window (see below) will show again, the blue fonts at the top left indicate whether you are Focused (good) or Unfocused (bad), and the percentage shows how confident the AI engine.

  1. Lastly, when you are unfocused for more than 10 seconds, a popup will display to remind you. You can dismiss it by clicking on OK, but you may want to get back to study mode!
