What makes Focul different

Personalized focus detection

Uses posture and micro‑movements (eyes, shoulders, face) to distinguish focused vs. unfocused — tuned to you.

AI‑powered

A multi‑layer perceptron learns subtle, personalized patterns you may not notice yourself.

Adaptive & ready

Ships with a starter model so you can use it immediately, then adapts as you record your own sessions.

Private by design

All data stays on your computer. No images stored, no network access, no tracking.

How it works

  1. Record 60s of your focused posture with the built‑in recorder.
  2. Record 60s of your unfocused posture (e.g. looking away, on phone).
  3. Click Train Model with Recordings — learning takes a few seconds.
  4. Start monitoring. Focul will gently remind you after ~10s unfocused.

Privacy first

Focul runs fully offline. We do not collect or transmit any data. Your personalized model is stored locally as numbers — not images.
